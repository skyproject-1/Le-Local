About Le Local

Leicester's trusted off licence & grocery delivery. Fast, reliable, and always fresh.

Our Story

Le Local was founded with a simple mission: to bring Leicester the best off licence and grocery delivery experience in the city.

We believe everyone deserves quick access to quality drinks, snacks, and everyday essentials - without leaving home. That's why we launched Le Local to deliver your favourite products straight to your door in just 20-30 minutes.

As a local Leicester business, we're proud to serve our community with the fastest delivery times, best prices, and exceptional customer service.

Our team is fully trained in age verification to ensure responsible alcohol sales, and we're licensed to deliver alcohol in Leicester.
