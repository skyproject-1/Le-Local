Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Le Local

What are your delivery hours?
We deliver 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Order before 11PM for same-day delivery.
How much is delivery?
Delivery is £2.99. Free delivery on orders over £20!
What is the minimum order?
Minimum order is £10. For alcohol orders, we may require age verification.
How long does delivery take?
We deliver in 20-30 minutes! That's our promise to you.
What areas do you deliver to?
We deliver across Leicester and surrounding areas. Enter your postcode at checkout to confirm.
What ID do I need for alcohol?
We accept Passport, Driving Licence, or Pass card. ID will be checked on delivery. You must be 18+.
Can I order alcohol for someone else?
Yes, but the recipient must be present and have valid ID. We cannot leave alcohol without ID check.
How do I track my order?
Click "My Orders" in the header to see your order status and estimated delivery time.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major debit and credit cards. Card payment on delivery or online.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, as long as it hasn't been prepared. Contact us immediately if you need to cancel.
